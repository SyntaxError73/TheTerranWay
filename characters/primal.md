# Primal

> “Would you like to do something together?”

---

## Overview

Primal is a Shifter — a being once thought mythical on Terra. Gentle, anxious, and deeply caring, he prefers peace and quiet over confrontation.

Despite his intimidating biology, Primal is one of the most emotionally considerate individuals Ashley and Amelia have met.

---

## Personality

- Calm and predictable
- Anxious about society
- Affectionate and attentive
- Speaks simply and honestly
- Becomes verbose only when stressed

---

## Traits

- Shapeshifter
- Prefers humanoid form to blend in
- Purrs when content
- Wags tail when happy
- Generates body heat intentionally

---

## Appearance Notes

- Dark skin
- Blue markings around eyes
- Short black hair
- Red dinosaur tail with black tip and retractable spines
- Retains raptorial claw even in humanoid form

---

## Relationships

- **Ashley** — partner; deep trust and affection
- **Amelia** — gentle caretaker dynamic
- **Hunter family** — cautious acceptance that grows into family
