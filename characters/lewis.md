# Lewis “Newt” Hunter

> “Okay, but what if—”

---

## Overview

Lewis Hunter, often called “Newt,” is the youngest of the Hunter siblings. Academically advanced from a young age, he is creative, talkative, and endlessly curious.

He is known for his imaginative thinking, love of animals, and tendency to spin absurd hypotheticals at the worst (or best) possible moments.

---

## Personality

- Energetic and talkative
- Easily distracted
- Deeply curious
- Emotionally sensitive beneath humor
- Creative enabler for others

Lewis thrives on ideas — not necessarily finishing them, but *starting* them.

---
