# Amelia

> “I promise I’m not mad. I’m just thinking.”

---

## Overview

Amelia is a Lamia from one of Terra’s major cities.  
She is shy, artistic, observant, and deeply sensitive to her surroundings.

She prefers quiet environments, structured spaces, and creative expression through digital art.

---

## Personality

- Timid and soft-spoken
- Highly empathetic
- Avoids confrontation
- Emotionally observant
- Clumsy and easily overwhelmed
- Relies on routines and familiar comforts

---

## Interests

- Digital art and photography
- Cozy games and puzzle games
- Libraries and quiet cafés
- Exotic meats (especially frog legs)
- Drawing emotional experiences through abstraction

---

## Health & Biology

- Lamia physiology
- Monthly shedding cycle that affects mood and energy
- Seasonal allergies
- Poor eyesight without glasses
- Experiences hypnagogic hallucinations
- One large lung and one vestigial lung

---

## Relationships

- **Ashley** — best friend and protector
- **Primal** — gentle caretaker presence
- **Hunter family** — chosen family
