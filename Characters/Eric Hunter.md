# Eric Hunter

> “You’re safe. I’ve got you.”

---

## Overview

Eric Hunter is the eldest of the Hunter siblings. Broad-shouldered, gentle, and deeply grounded, he serves as the emotional anchor of the family.

Though he was on the high school football team and carries an imposing physical presence, Eric is defined far more by patience, kindness, and quiet faith than strength.

---

## Personality

- Calm and steady
- Protective without being controlling
- Emotionally perceptive
- Strong sense of responsibility
- Rarely raises his voice
- Disapproves of cruelty, including toward oneself

Eric is often the one others turn to after things go wrong — not to fix them, but to *hold space* until things settle.

---

## Beliefs

- Strong Christian faith
- Interprets dreams as meaningful or symbolic
- Finds purpose in service and stability

---

## Relationships

- **Ashley** — younger sister; provides grounding and reassurance
- **Lewis** — younger brother; quietly protective
- **Jeannie & John** — parents he respects deeply
- **Amelia** — treated like family
- **Primal** — cautious respect, grows into trust

---

## Notable Traits

- Looks younger than his age (“baby face hasn’t fallen off yet”)
- Severe acid reflux / GERD; avoids citrus
- Strong handyman skills
- Works well with his hands
- Dislikes chaos, but tolerates it for family
